Who are we?

ATUS or @Us

We came up with this name as a group in less than a minute. If you are looking for quick decision makers then @Us is the team for you. Not wanting to waste time deliberating over a name, one team member said “throw names at us” with the quick reply being “@Us”. “What?”, “ @Us, you said throw names….”. As quick as that we agreed on our name!
